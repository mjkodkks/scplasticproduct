# Privacy Policy

**S.C. Plastic Products Co., Ltd.** ("the Company" or "we") is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) of Thailand. This policy is intended to explain how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

---

### 1. Data We Collect

We may collect your personal data through the following channels:
- Telephone
- Email
- Collection from third parties, such as search engines, social media, government agencies, or business partners

The types of data we collect include:
- Personal information, such as name, age, date of birth, nationality, and national ID number
- Contact information, such as phone number, email address, and postal address
- Account details, such as user accounts and usage history
- Identity verification documents, such as copies of national ID cards or passports
- Transaction and payment information, such as order history, credit card details, and bank account information
- Technical data, such as IP address or activity logs
- Other information, such as photos and related data considered personal information

**Note:** We do not collect data classified as "Sensitive Personally Identifiable Information" (SPII).

---

### 2. Purposes of Using Personal Data

We use your personal data to:
- Create and manage user accounts
- Deliver products or services
- Improve products or services, including your user experience
- Internal company use, such as performance evaluation
- Marketing and sales promotion (no additional data is collected for enhanced marketing)
- After-sales service
- Gather feedback
- Process payments for products or services
- Comply with the Company's terms and conditions
- Comply with laws and regulations of government agencies
